About gridsense

gridsense is an innovative platform that leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze and understand traffic patterns. Our mission is to make roads safer and traffic flow more efficient through intelligent automation.

Using cutting-edge computer vision technology, we can identify vehicles, pedestrians, and other objects in real-time, providing valuable insights for traffic management and urban planning.
